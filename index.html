Vegetarian Delights

Vegetarian stir-fry
A colorful mix of fresh vegetables like bell peppers, broccoli, carrots, and snap peas stir-fried in a flavorful sauce made from soy sauce, ginger, and garlic.
Caprese Salad
A classic Italian salad made with ripe tomatoes, fresh mozzarella cheese, and basil leaves. Drizzled with balsamic glaze and olive oil.
Eggplant Parmesan
Slices of eggplant coated in breadcrumbs, baked until crispy, and layered with marinara sauce and melted mozzarella cheese.
Vegetarian Chili
A hearty and flavorful chili made with a mix of beans, tomatoes, onions, peppers, and spices. Serve with toppings like avocado, cheese, and cilantro.

About Us

Our Journey

Founded on January 1, 2024, by the visionary Dasari Upendra, our hotel's rich history is defined by a steadfast commitment to quality in every aspect. From the outset, our primary goal has been to provide not just quality food but an abundance of it, ensuring our guests experience both satisfaction and indulgence. Our convenient pricing reflects our dedication to offering value without compromising on excellence. Dasari Upendra's high-level management skills have been instrumental in creating a clean environment that resonates with our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction. To ensure a personalized experience, we limit bookings, steering away from overcrowding and prioritizing individualized attention. As we look back on our journey, the fusion of these elements has shaped our hotel into a haven where patrons enjoy top-notch cuisine, impeccable cleanliness, and a commitment to exceeding customer expectations.

Our Commitment

Our hotel's commitment is in several key areas. First and foremost, we are dedicated to providing exceptional customer service marked by friendliness, efficiency, and personalized attention to meet the unique needs of each guest. Our commitment extends to maintaining impeccable standards of cleanliness and hygiene throughout the hotel, from guest rooms to common spaces. We prioritize guest safety and security, implementing robust measures and clear emergency procedures. Our accommodations are designed for comfort, ensuring well-maintained rooms equipped with necessary amenities. Environmental sustainability is a priority, with initiatives focused on energy conservation, waste reduction, and eco-friendly practices. Transparent pricing, accessibility, culinary excellence, and efficient check-in/check-out processes are integral aspects of our commitment. We engage with the local community, support local businesses, and participate in community initiatives. Prompt issue resolution, continuous improvement through guest feedback, staff training, and contributing positively to our surroundings are at the core of our commitments, ensuring a memorable and satisfactory experience for our guests.
